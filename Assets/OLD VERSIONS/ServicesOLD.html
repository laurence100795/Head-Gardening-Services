Services

If you require any of the services below please get in contact

General Garden Maintance

Would you like your garden to always look neat and tidy, then Head Garden Services can offer you a regular maintenance service so you always have a tidy garden to enjoy, while we do all the hard work!

Fencing

We can supply and install all kinds of garden fencing to meet your particular needs. All waste will be removed and correctly disposed of, leaving you with a tidy, newly fenced area.

Jet Washing

Jet washing can make shabby looking slabs look like new, and give you a smart tidy patio area to enjoy.

Turfing

Your old lawn will be removed and the knew lawn prepped and laid in the correct manner. You will be left with a new green and smart lawned area that turns into a lush lawn for you to enjoy in a matter of only a few days.

Borders

Have your borders lost their shape or looking rather messy? Then we can reshape them and make them look like new. If you have lawned area and you want some new borders, then Head Garden Services can instate them and then plant them to make them look amazing.

Hedges

Are your hedges getting over grown? If so, we at Head Garden Services will cut, shape, and tidy them up so you have a sharp and tidy garden.

Garden Clearance

Is your garden looking a bit old and tired and in need of some tender loving care? Then Head Garden Services can give it a new lease of life by tidying it up so it’s easier for you to maintain.

At HGS we treat each customer special providing quality and unique service to each individual.

Contact Head Gardening Services and it will be something you won't forget.
