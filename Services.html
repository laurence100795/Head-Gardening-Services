Service

If you require any of the services below please get in contact

General Garden Maintance

General Garden Maintance is a service I provide where HGS regulary come to your selected property and will do scheduled work on property.

Fencing

HGS will strip out all your fencing posting, remove and dispose of your old panel and redo your fencing for you.

Jet Washing

If you have a old stained patio, driveway or house walls, HGS can come and jet washing service for you. We can restore then back to there original radiant colouring as if there have just been installed

Turfing

If you have patchy and uneven grass that you would like evened out. HGS will clear out and level the ground ready for turfing and lay down new glass for your property. HGS also will do (the fake grass?) if you design that alternative

Shaping

Hedges

If you have overgrown and out of shape hedges HGS can reshape them for a cleaner and nicer look for your garden.

Garden Clearance

If you're garden is filled with unwanted Rubbish such as leaves, branches and general waste HGS can come and clear out your garden for you.
